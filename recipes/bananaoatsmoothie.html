Banana oat smoothie

A healthy drink to tag along with breakfast to start the day

This 3 ingredient recipe can be done in under 5 minutes

Ingredients List
  • 0.5 cup rolled oats
  • One banana
  • One cup milk
Recipe steps
  1. Add rolled oats to a blender and blend until the oats are the size of a fine crumb
  2. Add the banana and milk and blend well
  3. Pour in a glass
